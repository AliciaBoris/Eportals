About Us

At Eportals, we are dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap by offering affordable, quality computer training tailored for beginners, students, professionals, and everyone interested in building a career in tech fields.

Eportals Computer Training Center is a full service Computer Center registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission with registration number BN 3469159, established to provide top-notch Computer Training services in Abuja. With Qualified, passionate and dedicated Tutors, modern gadjets and a robust curriculum, Eportals is set to be the gateway to essential digital skills and career empowerment hub in Abuja.

Objectives

The objectives of our trainings are to enhance students’ understanding, knowledge and acquisition of:

  • 1. Basic concepts of computers and its operations;
  • 2. Manipulative, computational and problem-solving skills;
  • 3. Application of software packages;
  • 4. Operation of computer - related simple devices;
  • 5. On-line skills and their applications;
  • 6. Safe attitudes and good practices on effective use of computer;
  • 7. Potentials for higher studies in Computer related areas.
